ABOUT

Henry Zurawski is a Graphic and Web Designer working in Los Angeles. Originally from the Northwest Suburbs of Chicago, Henry has worked as a contract artist for over a decade and has experience with print, web, 3D drafting.

Skillsets

I am skilled in using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign with over a decade of experience. With experience and training in video game development and art, I am also well versed in Autodesk Maya and have working knowledge of Pixologic Zbrush. I also have working capability in front-end web development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript/jQuery.

Experience

I have worked as a freelance graphic designer with almost a decade of experience working with a variety of clients on both digital and printed projects. I am familiar with both screenprinting and DTG apparrel printing processes having worked in multiple shops as a graphic designer and printer. I also have experience working in tech as a QA Analyst for award winning game developer Naughty Dog and blueprint layout and processing for low voltage systems.

Education

With an Associates in Applied Sciences from Tribeca Flashpoint Academy in Chicago and a Design and Communication Arts Certificate from the University of California, Los Angeles, I have a broad range of training in multiple fields of digital art and design.

Passions

I am a dedicated designer and illustrator devoted to the improvement of my craft and skillset. I pride myself on utilizing my wide skillset to aid in creating the best art I can and ensuring no task is beyond the scope of my capabilities. I love to practice illustration and design in my free time to expiriment with new methods or build a library of self-made assets. I am an avid artbook collector and have a large collection dedicated to some of my biggest inspirations.
